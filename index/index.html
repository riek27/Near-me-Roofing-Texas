FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Roofing in Houston

Get answers to the most common questions Houston homeowners ask about roof replacement, repair, and maintenance.

How much does a new roof cost in Houston, TX?
The average cost of a new roof in Houston, Texas ranges from $5,000 to $25,000 depending on roof size, materials, pitch, and complexity. Architectural asphalt shingles are the most popular choice offering the best value. David Roof provides free, detailed estimates with transparent pricing — no hidden fees. We also offer flexible financing options including 0% interest plans.
How long does roof replacement take in Houston?
Most residential roof replacements in Houston are completed within 1-3 days. Larger homes with complex rooflines may take 3-5 days. Commercial projects vary based on scope. We provide a detailed timeline during your estimate.
Does David Roof offer free roof inspections in Houston?
Absolutely! David Roof provides complimentary roof inspections for all Houston homeowners. Our certified inspectors conduct a comprehensive 21-point evaluation using drone technology and thermal imaging. No pressure, no obligation — ever.
Is David Roof licensed and insured in Texas?
Yes. David Roof is fully licensed by the State of Texas and carries comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation. We are also GAF Master Elite certified — a designation held by only the top 2% of roofing contractors nationwide.
What roofing materials are best for Houston's climate?
Houston's hot, humid climate with severe storms demands durable roofing. We recommend: 1) Architectural asphalt shingles — best overall value. 2) Standing seam metal roofing — 50+ year lifespan, energy-efficient. 3) Impact-resistant shingles — rated for hail and high winds.
Do you help with insurance claims for storm-damaged roofs?
Yes — this is one of our specialties. David Roof provides full assistance with insurance claims for storm-damaged roofs. We handle documentation, photos, meeting with adjusters, and ensuring fair coverage. Many customers pay zero out-of-pocket thanks to our expert claim advocacy.
